Unique Weather Experience

As a 10 year old, visitng Philippines was a crazy experience. The weather is very different, when its hot, its humid and dry. But when it rains it pours. I remember one day it rained enough to the point of flooding the streets. I go outside and I see children younger than me taking a shower in the rain, meanwhile some teenagers were surfing on the flooded street.

Eating Great Food

Growing up in a Filipino household, our food is very nostalgic to me and hits a spot. Visting the Philippines and eating food homecooked tasted quite different compared to American food or on the street is an amazing experience. You really don't know good food is until you visit street markets in an Asian country.
